INJECT THE FUTURE

Without doubt, it is important to know about vaccination. Vaccine not only keeps the person alone from the disease, but also keeps the society away from deadly infectious diseases. The main ways that people can get involved is getting educated and spreading out what they know about vaccination. However, the published reports are usually long and hard to understand for normal people as well as hard to get access to. This is the reason that there are many individuals and groups who spread false information on the matter, using attractive representations such as images, videos or “clickbait” on social media. The lack of information is creating path for deadly diseases to come back, including plague, mumps or measles 0(“5 Old-Time Diseases That Are Making a Comeback.”).

CONCLUSION

The Greater Good

To help keep those diseases away, there needs to be more funded - organizations that dedicate their effort on spreading the honest information about vaccination, including its bad effects and the chances of having side effects. They need to create advertisements that are attractive and impressive so as to stay in the viewers’ minds for a long time. At the same time, they need to urge the governments and the scientists to publish full and honest reports on any type of vaccines that are going to be delivered.
