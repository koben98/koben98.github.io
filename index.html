About Me

Welcome to my e-portfolio. My name is Hoang Huy Nguyen, currently a senior at University of South Florida. At the moment, I am majoring in Computer Science and expecting to graduate in 2020. In recent years, I am fortunate enough to have chances to intern in many places specialized in different industries, including Government's Branch, Movie Industry, Hospitality and Education in Vietnam.

2019 ASTA Gobal Convention

In August 2019, following the Vietnam National Administration of Tourism delegation, I participated in the ASTA Global Convention 2019, which was held in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. Throughout the event, I had the chance to learn the professionalism in both organizing and attending professional events. During the convention, I got to meet with Head of Southern Division of Viettravel - Vietnam's leading travel agency as well as discuss with the Chairman of Vietnam National Administration of Tourism on different aspects of the industry domestically and internationally.





2017 Niagara University Undergraduate Student Research Conference



Entering freshman year, I had the opportunity to participate in Niagara University Undergraduate Student Research Conference with one of my early projects - TapHere. The application is to help drunk people avoid sending drunk texts/calls, while at the same time contact the owner's preset emergency contact with messages including the location and status of the phone owner. There is also an option to automatically book Uber/Lyft from current location to the preset destination. More information can be found in this link poster
