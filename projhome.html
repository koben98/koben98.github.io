ABOUT

Vaccination has been one of the most debatable topics recently. With the development of technology, people are able to have access to more sources of information online. As vaccine research is greatly developing, people are able to find more use of vaccines as well as create new vaccines for more diseases. While at the same time, there are more and more negative effects of vaccines are discovered

OUR MISSION

"To Inject The Future"

As professor Gaita, president of the Romanian Heart Foundation argues, “Now it’s time to focus on prevention”, avoiding illness is always better than spending time and money to cure it (Prevention Is Better than Cure). Due to that, vaccine was invented in order to prevent the risk of getting diseases. We strive our best to support what is best for the future of the world. With less diseases, human kind will be able to accomplish greater goals. We are all about the greater good.
